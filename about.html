Alchemy is committed to dynamic programming that explores old and new music, performed to the highest artistic standards. We undertake daring collaborations, experiment with use of space and movement, and create experiences to captivate and inspire. Above all we aim to connect with both urban and regional communities and share the joys of choral music, engaging with young and old audiences alike.
